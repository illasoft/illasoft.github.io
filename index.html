Black Heaven Saga

Demon Hunter Lukan Canary is sent on a mission to capture Snow, a former Soulfall Academia student, who is capable of summoning Demons. However, when Lukan discovers Snow isn't working alone, but with a Grand Summoner, the mission takes a deadly, more sadistic turn.

Description

Black Heaven Saga is a story-driven, turn-based JRPG with occultic themes and imagery.

Platforms: Windows, Mac OSX

Target Release Date: Fall 2018

Estimated Playtime: 6 Hours
